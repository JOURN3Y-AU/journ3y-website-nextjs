## 2. CONTRACT SUM AND PAYMENT

2.1 The Contract Sum for the Works is **$87,500** (excluding GST).

2.2 Payment shall be made within **45 business days** of receipt of a correctly rendered tax invoice.

2.3 The Principal Contractor may withhold payment if, in its **sole and absolute discretion**, it determines the work is defective or incomplete.

2.4 The Subcontractor agrees to a **10% retention** on each progress claim, to be held for **24 months** following practical completion.

---

## 3. VARIATIONS

3.1 No variation to the Works shall be valid unless **confirmed in writing by the Principal Contractor**.

3.2 The Subcontractor shall proceed with any variation directed by the Principal Contractor **immediately upon verbal instruction**. Written confirmation and pricing adjustment will follow within 14 days.

3.3 If the parties cannot agree on the value of a variation, the Principal Contractor's assessment shall be **final and binding**.

---

## 4. PROGRAM AND DELAYS

4.1 The Subcontractor shall commence work on **2 December 2024** and achieve practical completion by **20 December 2024**.

4.2 **Liquidated damages** of **$2,500 per calendar day** shall apply for each day beyond the completion date, with **no cap** on total liquidated damages.

4.3 Extensions of time will only be granted if the Subcontractor provides written notice within **24 hours** of becoming aware of a delay event.

---

## 5. INSURANCE AND INDEMNITY

5.1 The Subcontractor shall maintain:
- Public Liability Insurance: minimum $20,000,000
- Workers Compensation Insurance as required by law
- Professional Indemnity Insurance: minimum $5,000,000

5.2 The Subcontractor shall **indemnify the Principal Contractor against all claims, losses, damages, costs and expenses** arising out of or in connection with the Works, **regardless of any negligence or breach by the Principal Contractor**.

---

## 6. LIABILITY

6.1 The Subcontractor's liability under this agreement is **unlimited**.

6.2 The Principal Contractor's liability shall not exceed the **lesser of the contract sum or $10,000**, except for amounts required to be paid under clause 2.

---

## 7. TERMINATION

7.1 The Principal Contractor may terminate this agreement **at any time for convenience** by providing 24 hours written notice.

7.2 Upon termination for convenience, the Subcontractor shall only be entitled to payment for work **completed to the Principal Contractor's satisfaction** as at the date of termination. No compensation for loss of profit or overheads shall be payable.

7.3 The Subcontractor may only terminate this agreement if the Principal Contractor fails to make payment for **90 days** after the due date.

---

## 8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

8.1 Any dispute shall be referred to **arbitration** under the rules nominated by the Principal Contractor.

8.2 The costs of arbitration shall be borne by the Subcontractor unless the arbitrator determines that the Subcontractor's claim was wholly successful.

8.3 The Subcontractor waives any right to commence legal proceedings until arbitration has been completed.
